Analysis: Since there's actual weather data available, the tiles should show meaningful information, not solid colors.

Possible Issues & Solutions

  • Transparency: Weather tiles are often semi-transparent. On a white background, they may appear very faint.
  • Data Availability: Some layers may have no data for certain regions/times.
  • Color Scaling: Weather data might be rendered in very subtle colors.
  • Coordinate System: Tile coordinates might be incorrect for the Hong Kong region.
  • API Key Issues: The key might have restrictions or quotas.
